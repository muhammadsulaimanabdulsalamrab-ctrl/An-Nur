About An Nur

النور — The Light

What is An Nur?

A complete free Islamic companion: Qur'an (all 6,236 ayat via Quran.com), 99 Names, authentic hadith, stories, prayer times, qibla, and digital tasbih. Built as sadaqah jariyah.

Daily Content

Every day brings a new ayah, name, hadith, and story — automatically rotating from curated collections.

Sources

Qur'an: Quran.com API (Saheeh Int'l) · Hadith: Bukhari, Muslim, 40 Nawawi · Audio: EveryAyah.com · Prayer times: Aladhan · All authentic.

Free Forever

No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Single HTML file. Deploy anywhere. Open source.
