About An Nur

النور — The Light

What is An Nur?

An Nur (النور — The Light) is a free Qur'an discovery tool built as sadaqah jariyah for the Ummah. Find any ayah you half-remember by meaning, transliteration, surah, or theme — with audio recitation.

Audio Recitations

Audio is streamed from EveryAyah.com — a free, open resource. Choose from Mishary Alafasy, Abdul Basit, Maher Al-Muaiqly, or Al-Minshawi. Your reciter preference is saved automatically.

Features

48 curated ayat with Arabic, transliteration & translation · Full surah index (114) · 4 reciters · Audio playback · Bookmarks · Daily ayah · Topic explorer · Light & dark themes.

Coming Soon
