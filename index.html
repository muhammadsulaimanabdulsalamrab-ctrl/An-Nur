About An Nur

النور — The Light

What is An Nur?

An Nur is a free Islamic knowledge companion — Qur'an, the 99 Names of Allah, authentic hadith, and stories of the Prophets. Built as sadaqah jariyah for the Ummah. Updated daily.

Daily Content

Every day automatically features a new ayah, a Name of Allah, a hadith, and a Prophet's story — rotating from a carefully curated collection.

Sources

Qur'an: Saheeh International · Hadith: Bukhari, Muslim, 40 Nawawi, Riyad as-Salihin · Audio: EveryAyah.com · All authentic (Sahih/Hasan).

Free Forever

No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Open source. Just knowledge — given freely, as knowledge should be.
